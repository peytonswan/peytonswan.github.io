How AI Improves Support for Neurodivergent Individuals

Enhanced Learning Outcomes

AI tools like InnerVoice can improve language and communication skills for neurodivergent children, bridging educational gaps with neurotypical students.

Workplace Adaptation

Technologies like Salesforce's AI summarization help reduce cognitive overload, making professional environments more inclusive for neurodivergent employees.

Employment Practices

Companies such as EY are adapting hiring practices to accommodate neurodivergent talents, creating more inclusive work environments.
